GuysWhoCode

We are a nonprofit organization working to bridge the gap between students and computer technologies. We also aims at developing on ideas that are useful and beneficial to everyone.

Our mission

GWC is run by a group of tech enthusiastic students and computer science engineers who are really passionate about teaching computer technologies to students. Mentors at GWC inspire students to learn computer programming languages by exposing them to real life problems. We help students to utilize the cutting edge technologies in a unique and efficient manner.
